# Phase 6: Cave Floor Fixes

## Goal

Fix all cave floor visual issues: eliminate blank scroll space at stop 2 by moving the floor inside the sticky container, ensure edge-to-edge width coverage, prevent background objects from overlapping the floor at stop 1, reduce floor height by ~50% via CSS cropping, and add a soft fade on the top edge to eliminate the harsh straight line.

## Research Notes

### Current State

- **Stop 2 (parallax):** Cave floor is a separate `<div>` after the parallax `<section>`. The parallax section is 800vh tall but cards finish animating ~80% through scroll, leaving ~160vh of dead scroll before the floor appears. User must scroll through blank space to reach it.
- **Stop 1 (grid):** Cave floor is an `<img>` inside the section, below the grid. Works but has width gaps when viewport is wider than the SVG's natural aspect ratio (1370:508, ~2.7:1).
- **Static falling objects (stop 1):** Use `top` values from 5% to ~85% of the container. The cave floor at the bottom overlaps with objects in the lower range. Floor SVG is transparent so z-index alone won't fix it.

### SVG Details

- viewBox: `0 0 1370 508` (points, not pixels)
- `preserveAspectRatio="xMidYMid meet"` -- causes gaps on wide viewports
- Generated by potrace from bitmap -- complex path data (~80K tokens)

### Fix Strategy

1. **Stop 2 scroll-in:** Move cave floor inside the sticky container. Use `useTransform` to translate it from below the viewport upward as scroll progresses. Full opacity throughout (no fade). Appears after last card exits, using the existing dead scroll space as the reveal runway.
2. **Stop 1 width:** Use the SVG as a `background-image` on a fixed-height div with `background-size: cover` (or use `<img>` with `object-fit: cover` + `w-full`). Either approach guarantees edge-to-edge.
3. **Object containment (stop 1):** Cap the static slot vertical range so objects don't extend into the bottom area where the floor sits. Reduce max `top` from ~85% to ~65%.
4. **Height reduction:** Apply a fixed CSS height (~50% of natural) with `object-fit: cover` and `object-position: bottom` to keep the stalagmite tops visible while cropping excess height. Pure CSS, no image editing.
5. **Top edge fade:** Apply `mask-image: linear-gradient(to bottom, transparent 0%, black 15%)` to soften the harsh straight line at the top of the SVG.

### Scroll Math for Stop 2

Cards use `WINDOW_RATIO = 0.5`. With 5 cards, the last card's window ends at approximately scrollYProgress ~0.88. The sticky range runs from ~0.11 to ~0.89. The cave floor should translate from off-screen (100% below) to its resting position at the bottom of the sticky container over the range ~0.80 to ~0.95 (overlapping slightly with the tail end of the last card and extending into the post-card dead space). This turns the dead scroll into purposeful content.

**Critical constraint:** Do NOT modify `SECTION_HEIGHT_VH`, `WINDOW_RATIO`, or any card timing values in `animationConfig.ts`.
